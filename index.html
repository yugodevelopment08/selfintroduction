Hello, I'm Yugo!

I am a Product Manager at Manabie, currently leading the payment module for our Manabie ERP system. I specialise in transforming complex financial workflows into seamless, high-value user experiences.

With a bilingual (Japanese/English) perspective and an MBA from the University of Nottingham Malaysia, I blend strategic business thinking with a deep empathy for the end-user. My expertise lies in benefit-based feature development—ensuring that every line of code translates into a tangible advantage for the customer.

I thrive in fast-paced, international environments, collaborating daily with cross-functional development teams across Vietnam, Singapore, and the Philippines to ship products that matter.
